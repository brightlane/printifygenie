What is Printify?

Printify is a print-on-demand (POD) platform that allows you to create and sell custom products like T-shirts, mugs, hoodies, posters, and more without holding inventory.

You connect Printify to an online store (Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce), upload designs, and when someone orders, Printify prints and ships automatically.
